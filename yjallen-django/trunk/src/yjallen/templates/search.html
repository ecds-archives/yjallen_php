    Keyword searches within a letter.

    Author and title searches within a letter.

  • Author searches the letter senders. To view all the letters in chronological order, use the contents view.
  • Title searches find words in the letter titles. Searches work in the same way as keyword searches, but only search the titles.

    Wildcards
